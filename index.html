Picadillo

Picadillo has no one-hunder percent confirmed origin. It is a mexican cuisine,
coming from Mexico's independence from Spain in 1821 but could've been made much earlier.

Why No One Truly Knows Where Picadillo Originated. - Chris Sands.

Description

Brief and basic recipe which is also open to customization.

Ingredients.

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • ½ cup onion diced
  • 1 green bell pepper diced
  • 2 cups potato peeled and diced
  • 4 cloves garlic minced
  • 8 ounces tomato sauce*
  • 1½ cup beef broth
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 bay leaf

Directions

  1. Cook the ground beef most of the way, doesnt need to be fully cooked.
  2. Add in diced bell pepper, onion, and potatoes. Continue to cook over medium heat.
  3. Stir in tomatoes, beef broth and spices. Cook with lid on for 10 minutes.
  4. Cook an additional 10 minutes with the lid off. Most of the liquid will be absorbed or evaporate.
